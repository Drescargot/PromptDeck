# ⚡ Best practices for using prompts

To get the best results from this library, follow these simple rules.

---

## 🎯 1. Be specific with your situation

AI works better when it understands context.

❌ “Help me study”
✅ “I have a biology exam in 3 days and I struggle with memorization”

---

## 🧠 2. Use the prompt fully (don’t edit it too much)

Each prompt is designed carefully.

👉 Copy it as-is first  
Then adjust only if needed.

---

## 💬 3. Always add your real situation after the prompt

Example:

> “Here is my situation: I procrastinate a lot when studying math…”

This helps the AI adapt the prompt to YOU.

---

## ⚡ 4. Iterate if needed

If the answer isn’t perfect:
- re-run the prompt
- add more context
- clarify your goal

AI improves with better input.

---

## 🧭 5. Use the right category

Don’t use a creativity prompt for studying or vice versa.

Pick the closest category to your need.

---

## 🚫 Common mistakes

- Giving vague instructions
- Expecting one message to fix everything
- Not applying the advice
- Changing prompts too much

---

## 🚀 Pro mindset

Think of AI like a personal assistant:

The clearer your instructions → the better your results.

You are not just using AI.  
You are learning how to direct it.
