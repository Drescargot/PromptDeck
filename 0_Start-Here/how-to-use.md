# 🚀 How to use this library

Welcome 👋

This repository is a collection of ready-to-use AI prompts. You don’t need to code or understand AI to use them.

---

## 🧠 Step 1: Choose a prompt
Browse folders based on what you need:
- 🧘 Daily Life → motivation, habits, productivity
- 📚 Study → learning help, explanations, study plans
- 💼 Work → emails, focus, organization
- 🎨 Creativity → ideas, writing, content
- 💰 Business → entrepreneurship, money, strategy
- 🧠 Personal Growth → mindset, confidence, discipline
- 💬 Communication → writing, social skills

---

## 📋 Step 2: Copy the prompt
Open a file and copy the full “Prompt” section.

---

## 💬 Step 3: Paste into an AI
Use ChatGPT or any AI tool and paste the prompt.

Then add your situation.

Example:
> “I struggle to focus when studying and I get distracted easily.”

---

## ⚡ Step 4: Follow the output
The AI will respond as a coach, assistant, or expert depending on the prompt.

Act on it immediately for best results.

---

## 💡 Pro tip
Be specific when you talk to the AI.

❌ Bad: “Help me”
✅ Good: “I have 2 hours to study but I keep checking my phone”

---

## 🎯 Goal of this library
Make AI easy, practical, and useful for everyday life.
